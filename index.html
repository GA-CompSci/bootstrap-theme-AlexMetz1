Our Criteria

Offence

Offense is vital for top teams as scoring wins games and pressures opponents into mistakes. A strong offense boosts confidence, maximizes key players' talents, and helps control the game. In modern sports, offensive versatility is key to adapting and outpacing opponents.

Defence

Defense is crucial for top teams as it prevents scoring, disrupts opponents, and controls the game’s pace. A strong defense creates opportunities for counterattacks while maintaining stability and balance against high-scoring opponents.

Strength of Schedule

Facing tougher teams helps prepare for high-level competition and builds resilience. A challenging schedule can improve rankings, expose weaknesses, and strengthen a team’s ability to adapt under pressure.

Record

A strong record boosts confidence, improves rankings, and secures playoff or tournament spots. It also shows a team’s ability to win under various conditions, proving their competitiveness over a season.
